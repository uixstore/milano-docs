You can change your site's identity from **Appearance → Customize → Site Identity**.

## Logo

Upload your main logo from the **Logo** field. The logo replaces the site title in the header.

To add a retina (high-resolution) logo, use the **Logo (Retina)** field. Retina logos should be exactly twice the width and height of the standard logo.

## Site title and tagline

The **Site Title** and **Tagline** appear when no logo is set, and are used by search engines. Change them from the corresponding fields.

## Site icon

The **Site Icon** (also called a favicon) is the small icon that appears in browser tabs. Upload a square image, at least 512 × 512 pixels.
