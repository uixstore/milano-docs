Milano adds several panels to the WordPress **Customizer**, giving you full control over your site's look and behavior. Each panel contains settings grouped by feature.

## Global settings

- [Site identity](./site-identity/) — logo, site title, tagline, and site icon.
- [Colors](./colors/) — brand colors, text and link colors, and button colors.
- [Typography](./typography/) — body, heading, and button fonts.
- [Layout and containers](./layout-and-containers/) — content widths and corner radius.
- [Buttons](./buttons/) — button and input field border radius.
- [Pages](./pages/) — page header, background image, breadcrumbs, and height.
- [Custom CSS](./custom-css/) — add your own CSS.

## [Header](./header/)

The **Header** panel controls sticky behavior, logo, menus, categories bar, cart and account icons, search, and contact info. See [Header builder](../header/header-builder/) for detailed setup.

## [Footer](./footer/)

The **Footer** panel controls footer rows, columns, and copyright text. See [Footer builder](../footer/footer-builder/) for detailed setup.

## [Blog](./blog/)

The **Blog** panel controls blog archive layout, post cards, and single post settings. See [Blog overview](../blog/) for detailed setup.

## [Product Catalog](./product-catalog/)

The **Product Catalog** panel controls shop layout, product cards, category cards, and top categories. See [Shop layout](../shop-layout/) for detailed setup.

## [Product Page](./product-page/)

The **Product Page** panel controls product gallery, summary, tabs, reviews, and related products. See [Single product](../single-product/) for detailed setup.

## [Mobile](./mobile/)

The **Mobile** panel controls the mobile menu and bottom navigation bar. See [Navigation](../navigation/) for detailed setup.

## Store

The **Store** panel controls cart behavior, checkout settings, payment icons, and product badges. See [Cart & checkout](../cart-checkout/) for detailed setup.

To open the Customizer, go to **Appearance → Customize** in your WordPress admin.
