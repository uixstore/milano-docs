The **Product Page** panel in the Customizer controls the layout and behavior of single product pages.

## What you can set

- **Product Layout** — sticky summary, sticky add to cart, page header, product navigation (prev/next)
- **Product Gallery** — layout (slider, stacked, grid, or masonry), thumbnails position, zoom, lightbox
- **Product Summary** — visibility of overline, excerpt, meta, share, question button, sale countdown, live visitors, sold count, safe checkout, accordion tabs
- **Product Tabs** — enable or disable, tab style (horizontal, accordion, vertical)
- **Product Reviews** — standalone section or within tabs, rating summary
- **Related & Upsell Products** — enable, limit, columns, carousel mode
- **Recently Viewed Products** — enable, limit, columns, carousel mode
- **Product Badges** — sale, new, pre-order, and sold out badges with custom labels and colors

For detailed setup instructions, see [Single product](../single-product/).
