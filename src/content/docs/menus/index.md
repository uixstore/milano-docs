Menus control how visitors move around your store. You build them in WordPress and assign them to locations in Milano.

## In this section

- [Create a menu](./create-a-menu/) — build a menu from pages, links, and product categories, then assign it to a menu location.
- [Mega menus](./mega-menus/) — show rich content like images and columns inside your menu dropdowns.
