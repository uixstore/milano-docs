WooCommerce product tabs (Description, Additional Information, Reviews) appear below the product summary. You can turn them on or off and choose a style.

All settings live under **Appearance → Customize → Product Page → Tabs**.

## Turn tabs on or off

By default, tabs are enabled. Turn off the **Enable tabs** toggle to remove all product tabs entirely.

:::caution
If you disable product tabs and do not turn on standalone reviews, customers cannot see or leave reviews. See [Product reviews](./product-reviews/).
:::

## Tab style

Choose how tabs display on the page:

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD060 -->

| Style               | Description                                             |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Horizontal Tabs** | Default tabbed layout with clickable labels at the top. |
| **Accordion**       | Collapsible panels stacked vertically.                  |
| **Vertical Tabs**   | Labels on the left, content on the right.               |

<!-- markdownlint-enable MD060 -->

![Product page showing horizontal tabs with Description, Additional Information, and Reviews labels](../../../assets/shared/placeholder.svg)

## Accordion initial state

When using accordion mode, choose how panels open on page load:

- **Open first** — the first panel is open, others are closed.
- **Open all** — all panels are expanded.
- **Close all** — all panels are collapsed.
