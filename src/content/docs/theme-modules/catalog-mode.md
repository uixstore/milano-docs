Catalog Mode transforms your store into a read-only product catalog. Prices are hidden and the Add to Cart button is removed, making it impossible for customers to buy anything through the site.

This is useful for:

- Wholesale or B2B sites that take orders by phone or email.
- Stores that are temporarily closed.
- Product lookbooks where you want to show items without selling them online.

## Turn on Catalog Mode

1. Go to **Milano Dashboard → Modules**.
2. Find **Catalog Mode** and turn the toggle on.

## Configure

1. Go to **WooCommerce → Settings → Products → Catalog Mode**.
2. You can choose what to hide:
   - **Hide prices** — Removes all prices throughout the store.
   - **Hide Add to Cart** — Removes all Add to Cart buttons. Products say "Read More" instead.
   - **Hide both** — Full catalog mode with no pricing or purchasing.
3. **Exclude certain user roles** — Optionally let logged-in users (like administrators or wholesale customers) see prices and buy.
4. **Custom "Read More" text** — Change the text on the button that replaces Add to Cart.
5. Click **Save Changes**.

## Excluding specific products

You can exclude individual products from catalog mode from the product edit screen. Those products will show prices and the Add to Cart button as normal.

## Requirements

- WooCommerce must be installed and active.
