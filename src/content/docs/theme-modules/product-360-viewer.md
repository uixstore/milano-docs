The Product 360° Viewer lets customers rotate a product to view it from all angles. You upload a sequence of images (frames) showing the product at different rotations, and the viewer stitches them together.

## Turn on Product 360° Viewer

1. Go to **Milano Dashboard → Modules**.
2. Find **Product 360° Viewer** and turn the toggle on.

## Add a 360 view to a product

1. Edit a product.
2. Scroll to the **Product Data** meta box.
3. Find the **360° Viewer** section.
4. Upload your frame images:
   - Name the files in sequential order so they display correctly (for example, `product-01.jpg`, `product-02.jpg`, etc.).
   - Upload all frames at once.
   - You can reorder them by dragging.
5. **Update** the product.

A **360°** slide appears in the product gallery. Customers can drag or swipe to rotate the product.

## Tips for good results

- Use 24–36 frames for a full rotation.
- Keep the product in the same position across all frames. Use a turntable or a fixed camera setup.
- Export frames at the same resolution.

## Configure

Settings are under **WooCommerce → Settings → Products → 360° Viewer**:

- **Frame width** — The display width of the viewer.
- **Frame height** — The display height of the viewer.
- **Initial frame** — Which frame shows first (default: the middle frame).

## Requirements

- WooCommerce must be installed and active.
