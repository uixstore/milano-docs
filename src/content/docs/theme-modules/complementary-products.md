Complementary Products lets you hand-pick items that go well with each product. For example, on a dress product page you might show matching shoes and a handbag.

## Turn on Complementary Products

1. Go to **Milano Dashboard → Modules**.
2. Find **Complementary Products** and turn the toggle on.

## Add complementary products

1. Edit a product.
2. Scroll to the **Product Data** meta box.
3. Find the **Complementary Products** section.
4. Search for and select the products that pair well with this item.
5. Drag to reorder them.
6. **Update** the product.

The selected products appear in a carousel on the single product page, typically below the main content area.

## Configure the heading

1. Go to **Appearance → Customize → Single Product → Complementary Products**.
2. Change the carousel heading text (default: "Pairs well with").
3. Click **Publish**.

## Requirements

- WooCommerce must be installed and active.
