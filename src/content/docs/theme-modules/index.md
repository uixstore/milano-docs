Milano comes with modules you can turn on or off from the Theme Dashboard. Some add core theme features, others extend WooCommerce or work with Elementor. You'll find all of them under **Milano Dashboard → Modules**.

## In this section

### Theme modules

- [Mega Menu](./mega-menu/) — Build advanced menus with grid layouts, icons, and custom content.
- [Browser Tab Notifications](./browser-tab-notifications/) — Flash the tab title to re-engage visitors.

### WooCommerce modules

- [Product Card Swatches](./product-card-swatches/) — Show variation images on shop and archive pages.
- [Variation Compare](./variation-compare/) — Compare variations by attribute on product pages.
- [Ask a Question](./ask-a-question/) — Let customers ask about products from the product page.
- [Buy Now](./buy-now/) — Skip the cart and go straight to checkout.
- [Free Shipping Bar](./free-shipping-bar/) — Show how close customers are to free shipping.
- [Size Guide](./size-guide/) — Add size guide charts to products.
- [Product 360° Viewer](./product-360-viewer/) — Display an interactive 360-degree product viewer.
- [Product 3D Viewer](./product-3d-viewer/) — Display a 3D model in the product gallery.
- [Product Video](./product-video/) — Show product videos in the gallery.
- [Product Tabs](./product-tabs/) — Reorder, hide, and create custom product tabs.
- [Model Information](./model-information/) — Add model profiles and measurements to products.
- [Catalog Mode](./catalog-mode/) — Turn your store into a product showcase.
- [Complementary Products](./complementary-products/) — Show hand-picked "pairs well with" items.
- [Live Sales Notification](./live-sales-notification/) — Show fake purchase popups to build social proof.

### Elementor modules

- [Popup Builder](./popup-builder/) — Create targeted popups with Elementor.
