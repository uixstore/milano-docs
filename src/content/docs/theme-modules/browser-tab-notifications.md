Browser Tab Notifications flashes the page title in the browser tab when a visitor leaves your site. It's a subtle way to bring their attention back.

## Turn on Browser Tab Notifications

1. Go to **Milano Dashboard → Modules**.
2. Find **Browser Tab Notifications** and turn the toggle on.

The module is off by default.

## Configure

1. Go to **Milano Dashboard → Settings**.
2. Under **Tab Flash**, you'll find:
   - **Original text** — Leave this blank to use the page's original title. You can enter custom text to show when the tab is in focus.
   - **Flash text** — The text that appears when the visitor has left the page. For example: "Come back! We're running a sale."
3. Click **Save Changes**.

The tab flashes once and returns to the original title after a few seconds. It only triggers when someone switches to a different tab or window.
