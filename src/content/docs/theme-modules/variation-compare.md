Variation Compare adds a **Compare** button to variable product pages. When clicked, it opens a modal that shows all variations of a primary attribute (like Color) side by side so customers can compare them visually.

## Turn on Variation Compare

1. Go to **Milano Dashboard → Modules**.
2. Find **Variation Compare** and turn the toggle on.

## Configure

1. Go to **WooCommerce → Settings → Products → Variation Compare**.
2. Set:
   - **Primary attribute** — The attribute that drives the comparison (for example, Color).
3. Click **Save Changes**.

## How it works

On a variable product page, a **Compare** button appears near the attribute options. Clicking it opens a modal showing all variations grouped by the primary attribute, each with its image. Customers can scroll through and compare at a glance.

The comparison modal includes:

- Variation images at full size.
- The attribute label for each variation.
- A **Select** button to choose that variation and close the modal.

## Requirements

- WooCommerce must be installed and active.
- Variable products must have at least one attribute with images on the variations.
