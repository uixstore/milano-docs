The Product Video module adds a video slide to the product gallery. You can use self-hosted video files or embed from YouTube or Vimeo.

## Turn on Product Video

1. Go to **Milano Dashboard → Modules**.
2. Find **Product Video** and turn the toggle on.

## Add a video to a product

1. Edit a product.
2. Scroll to the **Product Data** meta box.
3. Find the **Product Video** section.
4. Choose your video source:
   - **Video file** — Upload an MP4, WebM, or OGV file. The video plays inline in the gallery.
   - **Video URL** — Paste a YouTube or Vimeo URL. The video embeds in an iframe.
5. **Update** the product.

A **Video** slide appears in the product gallery. Customers can click to play without leaving the page.

## Configure

Settings are under **WooCommerce → Settings → Products → Product Video**:

- **Autoplay** — Start the video automatically when the slide is active.
- **Loop** — Loop the video playback.
- **Mute** — Mute the video by default.

## Requirements

- WooCommerce must be installed and active.
- For self-hosted videos, your server must support the video file format you're uploading.
