Welcome to Milano — a multipurpose WooCommerce theme built for speed, flexibility, and ease of use. This section walks you through everything you need to do before customizing your site.

## The fast path — use the setup wizard

The fastest way to a working Milano site is the [setup wizard](./use-the-setup-wizard/). It opens the moment you activate Milano and walks you through five steps:

1. Enter your purchase code and accept the ThemeForest license terms.
2. Install the child theme.
3. Install required plugins (WooCommerce and Elementor).
4. Import a demo storefront (or apply Elementor settings only).
5. Confirmation screen with shortcuts to the dashboard and Customizer.

Total time: about 30 minutes. The wizard is the recommended path for most users. The wizard only opens once — to run it again, use **Milano → Dashboard → License → Re-run the Setup Wizard**.

## The manual path

If you want more control, or the wizard isn't right for your setup, follow each step by hand:

1. [Before you begin](./before-you-begin/) — check your hosting, PHP version, and download.
2. [Install Milano](./install-milano/) — upload and activate the theme.
3. [Register your purchase code](./register-purchase-code/) — license, updates, and support.
4. [Install the child theme](./install-the-child-theme/) — keep your customizations safe.
5. [Install required plugins](./install-required-plugins/) — WooCommerce and Elementor.
6. [Import a demo](../import-demo/import-a-demo/) — start with a pre-built storefront (optional).

## I skipped the wizard

Use the [first-time setup checklist](./first-time-setup-checklist/) to confirm nothing is missing. It covers the same six steps as a scannable list, plus the customization tasks that come after (logo, colors, menus, shop setup).

## What's next

Once Milano is installed and activated, you can:

- [Customize your site](../theme-settings/) — change colors, typography, layouts, and more.
- [Build your header](../header-builder/) — design a custom header with the drag-and-drop builder.
- [Set up your shop](../shop-layout/) — configure product pages, cart, and checkout.
