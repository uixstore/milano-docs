Use this checklist to make sure your Milano setup is complete. Each item links to the relevant guide.

## Checklist

- [ ] **Theme installed and activated** — [Install Milano](../install-milano/)
- [ ] **Purchase code registered** — [Register your purchase code](../register-purchase-code/)
- [ ] **Child theme installed and activated** — [Install the child theme](../install-the-child-theme/)
- [ ] **Required plugins installed and activated** — WooCommerce and Elementor. [Install required plugins](../install-required-plugins/)
- [ ] **Demo imported or building from scratch** — Choose one path:
  - [Import a demo](../import-demo/) — fastest way to get a working site.
  - Build from scratch — skip to the customization steps below.
- [ ] **Logo replaced** — set your logo and site title under **Appearance → Customize → Site Identity** _(guide coming soon)_.
- [ ] **Primary colors set** — adjust brand colors under **Appearance → Customize → Colors** _(guide coming soon)_.
- [ ] **Menus configured** — create a menu under **Appearance → Menus** _(guide coming soon)_.
- [ ] **WooCommerce set up** — configure store basics under **WooCommerce → Settings** _(guide coming soon)_.

## What to do next

Pick your path based on how you want to build your site:

- **Start from a demo** — go to [Import Demo](../import-demo/), pick a demo, and import it. Then customize it to match your brand.
- **Build from scratch** — open the Customizer under **Appearance → Customize** and start setting your colors, typography, and layout. Then use Elementor to build your pages. _(Customizer guide coming soon.)_
