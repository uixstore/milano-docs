Each blog post page has its own display options, all in the Customizer.

## Before you begin

Open **Appearance → Customize → Blog → Single Post** in your WordPress admin.

## Featured image

The featured image appears at the top of the post.

- **Show featured image** — turn this off to hide the image on the post page. It still shows on archive pages.
- **Featured image ratio** — choose the aspect ratio: 1:1 (square), 4:3, 16:9, or original.

## Post navigation

Post navigation links let readers move between the previous and next posts.

Turn on **Show prev/next navigation** to display links at the bottom of the post.

## Related posts

Related posts show readers more content from your blog.

Turn on **Show related posts** to display a grid of related posts at the bottom of the page. Milano selects related posts based on shared categories and tags.

## Share buttons

Share buttons let readers share your post on social media.

Turn on **Show share buttons** to display social share icons below the post content.

## Author bio

The author bio shows the post author's name and description.

Turn on **Show author bio** to display the author box below the post content. Edit the bio from the author's WordPress profile page (**Users → Profile**).
